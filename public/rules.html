Assassin Game Rule Book



The rules of the Assassin Game are simple. Once the game begins, every player will be assigned a target; when they eliminate their target, the target of the player they assassinated becomes their new target. This continues until there is only one assassin remainingâ€”the last living player is the winner.

If you are assassinated, you are out of the game.

- Assassinations must happen in a public place, i.e. not on any person's property. If your target lives in an apartment or dorm room, the actual dorms are off limits but the hallways, lobbies, etc. are fair game. If your target lives in a house, the sidewalks and streets are fair game but not their porch or living room.

- Assassinations can typically only be performed with a foam dart gun (i.e. Nerf) or water gun. This is sometimes subject to the organizer's rules; at conventions, for example, an assassination is usually completed by sticking a sticker on your target. Please be smart about this; don't use a weapon that will hurt your target or one that looks like a real weapon. Don't get yourself in trouble... or worse!

- There are no teams in the Assassin Game. Keep your target a secret from everyone!

It's a game, have fun! Nobody likes a killjoy.
